## How this file works

- **path** is either an exact file/line or a glob pattern (globs require a count — the lint must see that count not be exceeded).
- **type** is one of: `todo`, `fixme`, `xxx`, `hack`, `not-implemented`, `mock-return`, `feature-flagged-off`, `re-export-shim`, `deferred-stage`.
- **re-entry** is the concrete trigger that unblocks the stub (e.g. "Stage 6 GSTN sandbox integration" or "when CommunicationService.send_email lands").
- **expiry** is the calendar date after which the stub is auto-promoted to P1 regardless of the re-entry criterion.
- **approver** is the named individual whose written sign-off is on record.

New stubs NOT listed here are a defect. Any PR that introduces one without adding it here AND getting a named approver fails CI.
